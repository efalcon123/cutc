About Us

COMPUTER UPGRADE TECHNOLOGIES CO. (CUTC PH) is a local aspiring I.T. products reseller and service provider specializing in customized Management Information Systems for Accounting, Asset and Tagging Monitoring, Inventory, Payroll, Sales Marketing, Warehouse Management, and other systems.

Our goal is to streamline manual operations by offering tailored automated information systems that suit each businessâ€™s unique design model in a cost-effective manner without compromising efficiency and user-friendliness. We also provide professional consulting services on system design, development, maintenance, and integration of information systems into businesses' operations.

Founded in 2021 and fully operational as of April 2022, CUTC PH offers a comprehensive range of products and services, including I.T. hardware such as PCs, desktops, notebooks, barcode scanners, label printers, wireless and networking solutions, as well as supplies like label stickers and thermal receipt paper.

Headquartered at 24/F BGC Corporate Center, 30th St., corner 11th Ave., Bonifacio Global City, Fort Bonifacio, Taguig City 1634 NCR, Philippines. CUTC PH is committed to providing innovative solutions that enhance business efficiency and productivity through advanced technological offerings and expert consulting services.
